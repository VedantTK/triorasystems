Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Triora Systems different from other IT service providers?
We combine deep technical expertise with business acumen, delivering measurable outcomes rather than just technology implementations. Our certified professionals, proven methodologies, and 24/7 support ensure your success from day one.
How do you ensure the security of our data and systems?
We implement enterprise-grade security including zero-trust architecture, end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, and continuous monitoring. Our team holds CISSP, CISM, and other security certifications, and we maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance.
Can you help with both cloud migration and DevOps transformation?
Absolutely! We specialize in comprehensive digital transformation that includes cloud migration, DevOps/DevSecOps implementation, and cultural change management. Our holistic approach ensures all components work seamlessly together.
What is your typical project timeline and pricing structure?
Project timelines vary from 2-12 months depending on scope and complexity. We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price projects, time-and-materials, and outcome-based contracts. Contact us for a customized proposal based on your specific needs.
Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?
Yes, we offer comprehensive managed services including 24/7 monitoring, proactive maintenance, security updates, and continuous optimization. Our support packages are tailored to your specific requirements and SLA needs.

We have extensive experience with HIPAA, PCI DSS, SOX, GDPR, and other regulatory frameworks. Our compliance experts ensure all solutions meet industry-specific requirements while maintaining operational efficiency and security.
